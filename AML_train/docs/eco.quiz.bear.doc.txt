( CNN ) -- The largest freshwater fish ever caught was as big as a grizzly bear .

True

False

The answer is : True .

The biggest freshwater fish ever caught was a Mekong giant catfish found in northern Thailand in 2005 .
It was approximately nine feet long and weighed 646 pounds .
On average the grizzly bear will reach a height of six or seven feet and an adult male will weigh anything between 300 and 850 pounds .
A female grizzly however will only reach 450 pounds .

( Sources : National Geographic ; Defenders of Wildlife )
