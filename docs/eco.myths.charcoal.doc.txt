( CNN ) -- True or False : Activated charcoal is the best antidote for poison known to man .

The answer is : TRUE

Activated charcoal could save your life .
It was famously demonstrated in 1813 by French chemist Michel Bertrand , who swallowed 150 times the lethal dosage of arsenic trioxide ( one teaspoon ) -- and survived .
He had immediately consumed a 'liberal amount of charcoal ' following the arsenic and it saved his life .

Activated charcoal is known as a 'super absorber ' of poison and today it is the first-line treatment in the majority of poisoning cases .
