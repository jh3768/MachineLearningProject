Plane slides off Chicago airport taxiway - CNN.com
( CNN ) -- A Southwest Airlines plane slid off a snow-covered taxiway Friday at Chicago Midway International Airport in Illinois , but no one was hurt , aviation officials said .

A Federal Aviation Administration official said 98 people were aboard Flight 688 , departing for Los Angeles , California .

The plane became stuck in snow on the shoulder of the taxiway as it was heading to a runway , the Chicago Aviation Department said .

Passengers were taken off the plane and bused to the terminal for another flight , the department said .
The aircraft has been taken to the Southwest hangar .

The incident did not have an impact on operations because another runway was put to use almost immediately , the department said .
